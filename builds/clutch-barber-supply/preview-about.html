Our Story

Clutch Barber Supply started with a simple mission: give Dallas barbers access to the tools they need, when they need them. No waiting weeks for shipping. No wondering if the product is legit.

We opened our doors on Maple Avenue in 2019 with a small inventory and a commitment to knowing every product we sell. Today, we're proud to be Dallas's go-to source for premium barber tools, with thousands of barbers trusting us for their clippers, trimmers, and supplies.

But we're still the same shop at heart — run by people who understand what it's like behind the chair. We test the products. We sharpen the blades. We teach the classes. And we're here when you need us.
