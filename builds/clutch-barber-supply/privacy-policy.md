# Privacy Policy — Clutch Barber Supply

*Last updated: March 2026*

---

## 1. Introduction

Clutch Barber Supply ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website clutchbarbersupply.com (the "Site"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect information about you when you visit our Site or make a purchase from us.

By using our Site, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide Directly

When you place an order, create an account, sign up for our email list, or contact us, we may collect:

- **Contact information:** Name, email address, phone number, billing and shipping address
- **Payment information:** Credit/debit card details or other payment method info (processed securely through our payment processor — we do not store full card numbers)
- **Account credentials:** Email and password if you create an account
- **Communications:** Messages you send to us via contact forms, email, or phone

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you visit our Site, we automatically collect certain technical information, including:

- IP address and general location (city/region level)
- Browser type and version
- Operating system
- Pages viewed, time spent, and referral sources
- Device identifiers
- Cookie data (see Section 6)

### 2.3 Information from Third Parties

We may receive information about you from third parties such as:

- **Shopify:** Our ecommerce platform processes transactions and may share transaction data with us
- **Payment processors:** Stripe, PayPal, Shop Pay, or similar (subject to their own privacy policies)
- **Analytics providers:** Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, and similar tools
- **Email marketing platforms:** Klaviyo or similar, if you've subscribed to our email list

---

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- **Process and fulfill orders** — including shipping, tracking, and handling returns
- **Communicate with you** — order confirmations, shipping updates, customer service responses
- **Send marketing communications** — promotional emails, new product announcements, event invitations (only with your consent, and you can opt out at any time)
- **Improve our website and offerings** — analyzing traffic patterns, understanding what products are most popular, fixing errors
- **Prevent fraud and ensure security** — detecting suspicious activity, validating transactions
- **Comply with legal obligations** — tax records, law enforcement requests where required by law

We do not sell your personal information to third parties for their own marketing purposes.

## 4. Email Marketing

If you sign up for our email list (including via our 10% welcome discount offer), you'll receive promotional emails from Clutch Barber Supply. These may include:

- New product announcements
- Sale and discount notifications
- Event and class announcements
- Barber education content

**You can unsubscribe at any time** by clicking the "Unsubscribe" link in any of our emails, or by emailing us at info@clutchbarbersupply.com. We process unsubscribe requests promptly, typically within 10 business days.

We use your email data only for communications from Clutch Barber Supply. We do not share your email address with third-party marketers.

## 5. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We share your information only in the following circumstances:

- **Service providers:** We share data with companies that help us operate our business (Shopify, shipping carriers, payment processors, email marketing platforms). These providers are contractually obligated to protect your data and may not use it for their own purposes.
- **Legal requirements:** We may disclose information when required by law, court order, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of Clutch Barber Supply, our customers, or the public.
- **Business transfers:** If Clutch Barber Supply is acquired or merges with another company, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you via email or a prominent notice on our Site if this occurs.

## 6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our Site uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience.

**Types of cookies we use:**

- **Essential cookies:** Required for the Site to function (shopping cart, login sessions)
- **Analytics cookies:** Help us understand how visitors use the Site (Google Analytics)
- **Marketing cookies:** Used to deliver relevant ads and track campaign performance (Facebook Pixel, Google Ads)
- **Preference cookies:** Remember your settings and preferences

**Your cookie choices:**

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling certain cookies may affect your ability to use some features of the Site (such as maintaining your shopping cart). Most browsers also support opt-out tools from analytics providers.

To opt out of Google Analytics tracking, visit: https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout

## 7. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy, including:

- Order records: Retained for 7 years for tax and accounting purposes
- Account data: Retained while your account is active, or until you request deletion
- Email subscribers: Retained until you unsubscribe or request deletion
- Analytics data: Retained per the applicable platform's retention settings

## 8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

- **Access:** Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
- **Correction:** Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information
- **Deletion:** Request deletion of your personal information, subject to certain legal exceptions
- **Portability:** Request your data in a portable format
- **Opt-out of marketing:** Unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time
- **Restriction:** Request that we restrict certain processing of your data

**California Residents (CCPA):** California residents have additional rights, including the right to know what personal information is collected, the right to opt out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell personal information), and the right to non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights.

**EU/UK Residents (GDPR):** If you are in the European Union or United Kingdom, you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation, including the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at **info@clutchbarbersupply.com**.

No method of internet transmission or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.
