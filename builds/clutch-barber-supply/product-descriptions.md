## 1. BaByliss Pro FXOne Lo Pro Clipper (FX829)

The FXOne Lo Pro is BaByliss Pro's answer to the demand for a high-performance clipper that doesn't fight you on tight skin fades. The low-profile design keeps the blade closer to the surface, giving you a cleaner, more controlled angle on curved areas — temples, necklines, and around the ear especially.

**Motor:** Brushless high-torque motor with magnetic drive technology. Runs quiet, stays cool, and delivers consistent power throughout the cut without RPM drop under load.

**Blade:** Titanium-coated T-blade with zero-gap capability. Ships at a professional gap setting; adjustable for custom close work.

**Power:** Fully cordless with lithium-ion battery. Up to 2+ hours of run time per charge. Ships with a magnetic snap-on charging dock — no cable hunting mid-shift.

**Weight:** Lightweight build (~280g), balanced to reduce hand fatigue during long service days.

**Designed for:** Barbers focused on skin fades and precision detail work. The lo-pro head geometry makes it the go-to second tool for cleanups after a full-size clipper cut.

**What's in the box:** FXOne Lo Pro Clipper, charging dock, blade guard, cleaning brush, blade oil, charging cable.

**Pro Tip:** Use the FXOne Lo Pro as your go-to fade finisher after roughing in with a larger clipper. The low-profile head gives you unmatched visibility right at the skin — especially valuable on difficult temple curves.

**What's in the box:** Andis Master Cordless Clipper, rechargeable lithium battery pack, charging stand, blade guard, cleaning brush, blade oil, comb attachments.
