## FREE SHIPPING

**Orders over $300 qualify for free standard shipping** within the contiguous United States. Free shipping is automatically applied at checkout when your cart total meets the threshold.

Free shipping does not apply to expedited or overnight services.

## TRACKING YOUR ORDER

Once your order ships, you'll receive an email with a tracking number. You can use that number to track your shipment directly on the carrier's website or via the tracking link in your confirmation email.

If you haven't received a tracking email within 2 business days of placing your order, contact us at **info@clutchbarbersupply.com** or call **(214) 677-1059** and we'll look into it right away.

## SHIPPING ADDRESS

Please double-check your shipping address before completing checkout. We are not responsible for orders shipped to an incorrect address provided at the time of order.

**We currently do not ship to P.O. Boxes.** Please provide a physical street address at checkout.

## DAMAGED OR LOST PACKAGES

We take care in packing every order, but occasionally packages are damaged in transit. If your order arrives damaged:

1. Take photos of the packaging and the damaged item(s) before opening further.
2. Contact us at **returns@clutchbarbersupply.com** within **5 business days of delivery** with your order number and photos.
3. We'll file a claim with the carrier on your behalf and get a replacement or refund arranged promptly.

If your tracking shows delivered but you haven't received your package, contact us within **5 business days** of the marked delivery date. We'll investigate with the carrier and work to make it right.

At this time, we ship within the **contiguous United States only**. We do not currently offer international shipping.
