# FAQ — Clutch Barber Supply

---

## SHIPPING QUESTIONS

### 1. How long does shipping take?
Standard orders typically arrive within 3–7 business days of shipment. Orders placed before our daily cutoff time usually ship the same business day. You'll receive a tracking number via email as soon as your order is on its way.

### 2. Do you offer free shipping?
Yes. Orders over **$300** qualify for free standard shipping within the contiguous United States. The discount is applied automatically at checkout — no code needed.

### 3. Do you ship internationally?
Currently, we ship within the contiguous United States only. We're working on expanding — check back or join our email list to be notified when international shipping becomes available.

### 4. Can I change my shipping address after placing an order?
If your order hasn't shipped yet, contact us immediately at **(214) 677-1059** or **info@clutchbarbersupply.com** and we'll do our best to update it. Once an order has shipped, we're unable to change the destination.

### 5. My tracking says delivered, but I didn't receive my package. What do I do?
Contact us within 5 business days of the marked delivery date at **info@clutchbarbersupply.com**. We'll investigate with the carrier and work to resolve it quickly.

## RETURN QUESTIONS

### 6. What's your return policy?
We accept returns on **unused, unopened items in original packaging within 30 days of delivery**. To start a return, email **returns@clutchbarbersupply.com** with your order number. Full details are on our [Return Policy](/policies/refund-policy) page.

### 7. My clipper/trimmer arrived defective. What do I do?
Contact us at **returns@clutchbarbersupply.com** within 30 days of delivery. Include your order number and a quick description of the issue (photos are helpful). We'll send you a prepaid return label and arrange an exchange or store credit — no charge to you.

### 8. Can I return a clipper I've already used?
Items that have been opened and used cannot be returned for a refund unless they have a defect or malfunction. For defective used items, we'll work with you on a case-by-case basis. For warranty issues on tools you've had longer, we can help connect you with the manufacturer's warranty support.

## PRODUCT AUTHENTICITY QUESTIONS

### 9. Are your products genuine / authorized?
**Yes — 100%.** Clutch Barber Supply is an authorized dealer for every brand we carry, including BaByliss Pro, JRL, Andis, Wahl, Gamma | StyleCraft, and others. All products are sourced through authorized distribution channels, which means:

- Valid manufacturer warranties apply
- Serial numbers are registered and trackable
- You're protected under the brand's standard warranty terms

We do not sell grey market, counterfeit, or unauthorized goods.

### 10. Do the clippers I buy from you come with a manufacturer warranty?
Yes. Because we're an authorized dealer, the manufacturer's warranty applies to every new tool you purchase from us. Warranty terms vary by brand — we're happy to help you understand the warranty on a specific product. Email or call us and we'll get you the details.

### 11. How do I know which clipper is right for my kit?
That's exactly what we're here for. Call or text us at **(214) 677-1059**, stop by the store in Grand Prairie, or email **info@clutchbarbersupply.com** with a description of what you're looking for (skin fades, scissor work, all-around cuts, etc.) and we'll point you to the right tool. We know these products.
