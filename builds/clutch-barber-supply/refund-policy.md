# Return & Exchange Policy — Clutch Barber Supply

---

## OUR COMMITMENT

At Clutch Barber Supply, we want you to feel confident every time you shop with us. If something isn't right, we'll make it right — straightforwardly and without hassle.

---

## 30-DAY RETURNS

We accept returns on **unused, unopened items in original packaging** within **30 days of delivery**.

To start a return:
1. Email us at **returns@clutchbarbersupply.com** with your order number and the item(s) you'd like to return.
2. We'll reply within 1–2 business days with return instructions.
3. Once we receive and inspect the item, your refund will be processed within 5–7 business days to your original payment method.

**Return shipping costs** are the customer's responsibility for change-of-mind returns. We recommend using a trackable shipping service, as we cannot guarantee receipt of items without tracking.

---

## DEFECTIVE OR DAMAGED ITEMS

If your item arrives defective, damaged, or not as described, we've got you.

- Contact us within **30 days of delivery** at **returns@clutchbarbersupply.com** with your order number and a brief description (photos help if available).
- We'll send you a **prepaid return shipping label** at no cost to you.
- We'll offer your choice of a **full exchange for the same item** or **store credit** for the full purchase amount.

We stand behind every product we sell and every brand we carry.

---

## OPENED / USED ITEMS

We understand that professional tools get put to work — sometimes immediately. For items that have been opened and used but are **experiencing a defect or malfunction**, we'll work with you on a case-by-case basis to find the right solution, including exchange or store credit.

Items that have been used and are functioning as intended are not eligible for return or exchange, but we're always happy to help troubleshoot or connect you with manufacturer support for warranty claims.

---

## NON-RETURNABLE ITEMS

The following items are not eligible for return:
- Event tickets and class registrations (TDLR CE classes, masterclasses)
- Downloadable or digital products
- Items marked **Final Sale**

If you have a question about whether a specific item qualifies for return, email us before purchasing — we'll give you a straight answer.

---

## EXCHANGES

Want a different size, color, or product? We're happy to help with exchanges on unused, unopened items. Contact us at **returns@clutchbarbersupply.com** and we'll walk you through the process.

---

## REFUND TIMELINE

- Refunds are processed within **5–7 business days** of receiving your returned item.
- You'll receive an email confirmation when your refund has been issued.
- Depending on your bank or card provider, it may take an additional 3–5 business days for the funds to appear in your account.
