## Design System Applied

**Colors:**
- Background: #05070c (dark), #091018 (light), #0d1822 (lighter)
- Text: #f5efe3 (primary), #d1c7b3 (muted)
- Accent: #c9894b (copper)

**Typography:** DM Sans throughout

**Copper Glow:** Radial gradients (20-45% opacity) on every major section

**Components:**
- Cards: 16-20px border radius, lift on hover
- Buttons: Copper primary, ghost secondary
- Forms: Dark inputs with copper focus states
- Filters: Sidebar desktop, drawer mobile
