# Straight Point Marketing — Cold Caller Training System
## Overview

This is the full onboarding and training system for cold callers at Straight Point Marketing. You're calling landscaping business owners across the US on behalf of our clients. The goal is simple: get qualified decision-makers on a call to discuss our lead gen service.

You don't need to be perfect. You need to be consistent, coachable, and present on every call. The callers who win here are the ones who put in the reps and actually apply feedback — not the ones who think they already know it all.

Use this system exactly as laid out. Don't skip ahead.

---

## What You're Calling For

Straight Point Marketing runs paid Facebook/Instagram ads for landscaping companies. We generate leads — homeowners looking for landscaping work. Our pitch to landscapers is simple:

**"We run ads, generate leads, and only charge you when we deliver. You get a qualified homeowner who's ready to buy, we get paid per appointment."**

Your job on the cold call is NOT to close. Your job is to get the owner curious enough to hop on a call with Andrew.

## 3-Day Ramp Plan

### Day 1 — Foundations (No Calls)

**Morning (1–2 hrs):**
- Read `training-overview.md` front to back
- Read `reading-the-call.md` — don't try to memorize it yet, just absorb it

**Afternoon (1–2 hrs):**
- Read `objection-scripts.md` out loud — yes, out loud
- Do the "What Bucket Is This?" drill at the end of `reading-the-call.md`
- Do the Tone Recognition drill

**End of Day:**
- Pick 3 objections from `objection-scripts.md` and write out how you'd handle each one in your own words
- Send to Andrew for review

**Day 1 Goal:** You understand the framework. You know the 4 buckets. You can identify a tone.

---

### Day 2 — Script + Shadow

**Morning:**
- Do the 10-minute warm-up from `daily-practice-drills.md`
- Review the call script (provided separately in GHL)
- Practice the opener 10x out loud — record yourself on your phone, listen back

**Afternoon:**
- Shadow Andrew on 2–3 live calls (listen only, no speaking)
- After each call, fill out the annotation template from `call-annotation-guide.md` on your own
- Compare your annotations with Andrew's debrief

**End of Day:**
- Do a 3-minute role play with Andrew using the format from `daily-practice-drills.md`
- Andrew gives pass/fail on each section

**Day 2 Goal:** You've heard real calls. You can annotate what happened. You can run the opener clean.

---

### Day 3 — Role Play + Go Live

**Morning:**
- Full 10-minute warm-up
- 3 role play scenarios with Andrew: warm prospect, guarded prospect, hostile prospect
- Must pass all 3 before going live

**Afternoon:**
- First live calling session — 20–30 dials
- Andrew listens to your first 5 calls (or pulls recordings)
- Debrief after the session using the annotation guide

**End of Day:**
- Self-review: listen to your best and worst call from the session
- Fill out annotation template for both
- Write down 2 things you did well and 1 thing to fix tomorrow

**Day 3 Goal:** You've made real calls. You've gotten real objections. You know what to work on.

## How to Use These Materials After Day 3

**Every calling day:**
1. Do the 10-minute warm-up from `daily-practice-drills.md`
2. Review one objection you struggled with yesterday
3. Call

**Weekly:**
- Andrew pulls 2–3 recordings and annotates them using `call-annotation-guide.md`
- Team debrief — what patterns are showing up?
- Check your weekly benchmarks from `daily-practice-drills.md`

**When you freeze on a call:**
- After the call, figure out which bucket that objection was in
- Find the response in `objection-scripts.md`
- Say it out loud 5x before your next session

**When you're struggling with a specific objection:**
- Go back to `reading-the-call.md` — understand the bucket first, then go to the script
- If you understand *why* the response works, you can adapt it on the fly

---

## What "Ready" Looks Like

You're ready to operate without Andrew on the phones when:

- [ ] You can identify any objection's bucket within 3 seconds
- [ ] You have 2 responses memorized for every common objection
- [ ] You can read tone from the first sentence and adjust without thinking
- [ ] Your connect-to-conversation rate is improving week over week
- [ ] You've hit weekly benchmarks for 2 weeks straight

---

## A Note on Mindset

You're going to get rejected. A lot. That's the job. The owner doesn't hate you — he's just busy, skeptical, and gets 10 calls like this a week. Your job is to be the one who doesn't fold the second it gets uncomfortable.

The difference between a caller who books meetings and one who doesn't isn't talent — it's reps. Put in the reps. Use the feedback. Get better every day.

Let's go.
